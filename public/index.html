Our Services

What we can do for you

Sleep Quality

DreamLight tracks sleep quality using body temperature, movement, and body temperature. It measures sleep duration, detects deep and light sleep cycles, and identifies disturbances. AI analyzes patterns to flag potential sleep disorders like insomnia or apnea. Personalized recommendations help users improve sleep habits for better rest and overall health.

Activity

DreamLight tracks physical activity using its built-in accelerometer, measuring movement, intensity, and duration. It detects workouts, daily steps, and activity trends, providing real-time feedback. AI analyzes patterns to suggest improvements, helping users stay active. Whether for fitness goals or general health, DreamLight optimizes activity levels for a healthier lifestyle.

Thermometer

DreamLight monitors body temperature in real time, detecting fluctuations that may indicate fever, overheating, or illness. AI analyzes trends to alert users if they’re getting sick or need to cool down. By providing early warnings and personalized recommendations, DreamLight helps maintain optimal body temperature for better health and well-being.

Lighting

DreamLight tracks environmental lighting to optimize physical and mental health. It detects light exposure levels, helping users balance time in natural and artificial light. AI provides insights on improving sleep, mood, and energy by encouraging healthier lighting habits. Proper light exposure enhances circadian rhythms, boosts focus, and supports overall well-being.
